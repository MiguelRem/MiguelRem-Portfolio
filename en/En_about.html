Welcome to my Portfolio!

My name is Miguel Teixeira, and I’ve been a Portuguese Multimedia Designer since 2018. My journey started at the Instituto das Artes e da Imagem in Vila Nova de Gaia.

After that, I continued my studies at ESMAD – Escola Superior de Media Artes e Design, in the same multimedia field, while focusing mainly on digital game development and 3D modeling.

I still consider myself a Junior Programmer and a beginner 3D Artist who mainly uses Unity Engine and Blender as my main work tools.

Although my main focus is Digital Games, I have developed skills in other areas such as Photography, Videography (and editing for both), 3D Animation, and Motion Graphics.

I’m also very communicative and full of energy, which greatly helps me connect with people who inspire me to grow in this field!
