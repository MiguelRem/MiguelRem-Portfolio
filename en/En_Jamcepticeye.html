Death by a Thousand Gears

Game developed during the Jamcepticeye in collaboration with Fancy Crab Studios. During this Game Jam with the theme "Death is an Opportunity," we created a Boss Rush game where every time the player died, they had to recover their body to collect a currency used to upgrade the character.

In this project, I was responsible for developing basic systems and mainly for the creation and modeling of environments.
